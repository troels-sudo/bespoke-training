Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Bespoke Training Dubai (“we”, “us”, “our”) values your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you interact with our services.

1. Data We Collect

We may collect your name, contact details, and information shared during your application, consultation, or training sessions. All data is stored securely and used solely for service delivery.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to provide personalized training programs, manage scheduling, process payments, and maintain communication regarding your coaching progress.

3. Payment Security

All online payments are processed through Stripe’s encrypted platform. We do not store your card details.

4. Third-Party Access

We do not sell or rent your data. Access is limited to our team and trusted partners directly involved in your service delivery.

5. Data Retention

Your personal data will be retained only as long as necessary to fulfill service obligations or as required by UAE law.

6. Contact

For any privacy inquiries, please contact us at info@bespoketrainingdubai.ae.
