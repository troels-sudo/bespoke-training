Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how Bespoke Training Dubai, operated by Into The Room Personal Coaching LLC (“we”, “our”, or “us”), collects, uses, and protects personal information provided by clients and visitors.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us through our application form, contact forms, or email communication. This may include your name, contact details, goals, and relevant health information necessary for program design.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information is used solely for communication, scheduling, program development, and payment processing. We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with any third parties unrelated to service delivery.

3. Payment Security

Payments are processed securely via Stripe. We do not store or have access to your credit card information. All transactions are protected by industry-standard encryption.

4. Data Retention

Client data is retained only for the duration of the coaching relationship and administrative compliance. You may request data deletion at any time by contacting us.

5. Cookies and Analytics

Our site may use basic, privacy-friendly analytics tools to understand engagement. No personally identifiable tracking or advertising cookies are used.
