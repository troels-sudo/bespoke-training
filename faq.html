Frequently Asked Questions

Your most common questions — answered with transparency and detail.

Who is Bespoke Training Dubai for?

Executives, founders, and professionals seeking measurable performance improvement within a structured, private environment. We work exclusively one-to-one and by application only.

Where are you located?

Inside Into The Room at Dubai Hills. The facility is minutes from Dubai Hills Mall and central to most residential areas.

How private are sessions?

100% private. Only one client trains at a time — no shared space, no onlookers. Every session is booked discreetly and by appointment only.

Do you offer nutrition or recovery support?

Yes. All clients receive nutritional guidance aligned with training objectives. Recovery protocols, including sleep tracking, breathwork, and mobility, are integrated when relevant.

Can I train while traveling?

Absolutely. You’ll receive travel protocols adaptable to any hotel gym or environment. Your program remains continuous, not interrupted by geography.

What is the investment?

Private 12-week intensives start at AED 49,000 and include full diagnostics, training, and ongoing data review. A refundable AED 1,800 deposit secures your consultation.

How do I apply?

Click Apply to complete your short application. We respond within 24 hours and, if suitable, provide a deposit link to confirm your consultation.

Still have questions?
